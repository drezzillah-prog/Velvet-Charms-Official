Welcome to Velvet Charms — handmade with care

At Velvet Charms we make cozy, heart-warming handmade treasures — candles, soaps, knitted winterwear, paintings, epoxy home pieces and small baubles designed to become little keepsakes in your life.

Our approach

Everything you see is made with attention to detail and a love for texture: slow-crafted candles with sculptural reliefs, soaps hand-cut and fragranced with essential oils, knitted winter sets brushed for ultimate softness, and mixed-media artworks that combine paint and clay for depth and personality. We source gentle materials and prioritize safe, skin-friendly formulations for our creams and soaps.

Personalized commissions

We love commissions — portraits, a special mug, or a bespoke set for a gift. When you order a custom piece you can:

  • Choose materials and scents
  • Send reference photos or sketches
  • Request specific colors, initials, or messages

Use the product customization box on the product page to add notes and upload one supporting file (image or PDF). If you need to upload more files, message us on Instagram or use the contact form and we’ll provide an upload link. For professional commissions we always confirm details before starting (design > mockup > final), ensuring you love the result.

Shipping & lead times

Most ready-made items ship within 3–7 business days. Custom orders depend on complexity — small commissions usually take 1–2 weeks, larger or sculptural pieces up to 3–4 weeks. If you need a guaranteed delivery date, message us before ordering and we’ll confirm feasibility.
